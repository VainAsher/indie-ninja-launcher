# Gameplay Guide

## Game Modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Campaign** | Story-driven mode with 30 missions across 6 regions. Unlock abilities as you progress. |
| **Arcade** | Endless procedural worlds with no story. Run as long as you can. |
| **Sandbox** | Free exploration with all abilities unlocked. |

---

## Campaign Overview

Progress through 6 regions in order. Each region unlocks after completing the previous one.

Regions: Forest → Town → Caves → Castle → Sewers → ???

Each region has:
- Regular missions (exploration, combat, objectives)
- An optional boss mission
- An NPC who gives context and side tasks
- A shop for upgrades

**Ability gates**: some regions require specific abilities to access (e.g. Town portal requires double jump). Earn abilities by completing missions.

---

## Core Movement

**Ground movement**: High acceleration — the ninja responds instantly. Move left/right to accelerate, release to decelerate quickly.

**Jumping**: Multiple jump types are available depending on your unlocked abilities:

- **Ground jump** — tap Jump from the ground
- **Coyote time** — 0.12s grace period after walking off a ledge
- **Jump buffering** — pre-press Jump up to 0.14s before landing
- **Double jump** (unlockable) — press Jump again in the air
- **Wall jump** — press Jump while against a wall for a horizontal boost

**Dash**: Tap Shift to dash. Covers distance quickly and cancels on wall contact. Short cooldown.

**Crouch**: Toggle stealth mode. Moves slower but fits through gaps. Can jump from crouch (reduced height).

**Fast fall**: Hold Down while airborne to fall faster. Useful for landing quickly.

---

## Combat

**3-hit combo**: Tap Attack up to 3 times for a ground combo. Each hit deals damage.

**Air attack**: Attack while airborne for a downward slash.

**Dash attack**: Attack during a dash for a fast horizontal strike.

**Shuriken**: Throws a projectile horizontally. Costs nothing, short delay between throws.

**Teleport** (unlockable): Short-range teleport in the direction you're moving.

**Ninjutsu** (unlockable): Special ability — varies by progression.

---

## Enemies

Five enemy types with different AI behaviours:

| Enemy | Behaviour |
|-------|-----------|
| Slime | Slow patrol, basic contact damage |
| Bat | Flying, chases on sight |
| Skeleton | Patrol and melee attack |
| Orc | Aggressive chase, high HP |
| Demon | Ranged attacks, phases |

Enemies drop coins and occasionally items on death.

---

## Shops & Inventory

Find shops in hub areas and some missions. Three tiers:
- **Basic** — health items and common upgrades
- **Advanced** — ability upgrades and rare items
- **Master** — rare and legendary items

Buy with coins collected from enemies and treasure chests.

---

## Save System

Progress saves automatically after completing each mission. There is no manual save.

If you quit mid-mission, you restart that mission from the beginning (progress within a mission is not saved).

---

## Tips

- Use coyote time and jump buffering for tight platforming — they're generous by design
- Dash through enemy attacks to avoid damage
- Crouch under projectiles and low obstacles
- Wall jump is available even without the double jump ability
- Shuriken can hit enemies through walls and around corners
