# Indie Ninja Adventures

**A fast-paced 1–4 player Metroidvania platformer by Vain Asher Gaming.**

Fight your way through 30 missions across 6 regions. Master ninja movement — dash, wall-jump, shuriken, teleport — and take on bosses, NPCs, and procedurally generated worlds. Play solo or with up to 3 friends online.
